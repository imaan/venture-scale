## Category Context
- **Existing Practices:** VCs review decks manually, use internal scorecards/checklists, sometimes use junior analysts to pre-screen. Some use AI tools but quality varies widely. Most pitch deck "AI analyzers" are founder-facing (help you build a better deck), not investor-facing (help you evaluate faster).
- **Market Signals:** AI tooling for VCs is emerging but most is CRM/deal-flow focused (Affinity, Harmonic, etc.), not deck analysis. The investor-facing pitch analysis niche may be underserved.
