Key Insights
The Real Competitor

DIY AI is the real competitor. With 85% of VCs already using AI daily, the primary alternative is a VC pasting a deck into ChatGPT. The "why not just prompt it?" question is the central competitive threat. Answer: consistency, stage-gating, structured scoring.

Structural Advantage

COGS are structurally low because the product is pure inference -- no data enrichment, no CRM sync, no proprietary datasets. Margins stay high at almost any price point. The question is entirely about willingness-to-pay, not cost structure.

Where Value Concentrates

Enterprise platforms own the durable power. Affinity (50% of top VCs), Harmonic ($30M raised). Once deal flow lives in Affinity, switching costs are enormous.

Human analysts retain irreplaceable judgment. $78/hr avg -- a VC analyst spending 5-10 hrs/week on screening = $20K-$40K/yr in labor cost allocated to the exact task VS automates.

Venture Scale's entry layer has zero lock-in. Per-analysis tools are easily substitutable. The framework IP and output consistency are the only defensible moat at this layer.

Kill / Go Criteria
Kill Signal

Nobody uses it after sending to 15 VCs. The form factor is wrong -- go back to discovery. Don't iterate on a product nobody opens.

Go Signal

5+ repeat users analyzing 3+ decks each. Build more features. Batch upload is the first paid feature.

When to Charge
1

Free during alpha. Remove all friction. The goal is learning, not revenue.

2

Ask about payment when feature requests come in. "Would you pay for batch upload?" tells you if willingness to pay is real.

3

$5/mo start, grandfather early users. Price up as features are added. Market rate is $30-$50/mo.

The Strategic Fork (After Alpha)
Path A: Relationship Opener Works

VCs respond, take meetings, some become regular users. Optimize for relationship quality, not revenue. Keep it free or very cheap. The product is a networking tool with a small SaaS revenue stream.

Path B: Product Is Genuinely Useful

VCs use it regularly on real deal flow. Associates integrate it into screening workflow. Price at market rates ($30-$50/mo), invest in features (batch, CRM, team), build a real SaaS business.
