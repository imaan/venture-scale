## Strategy
- **Product:** Venture Scale
- **Hypothesis:** I believe VC associates and partners who screen high volumes of pitch decks will find this useful because it turns 3-5 minutes of manual evaluation into a structured, consistent analysis in under 60 seconds — and every analysis forwarded to a colleague becomes a product demo.
- **Ideal User:** A VC associate screening 50-200 decks per month who needs to quickly separate the fundable from the clearly-not, and produce structured summaries for partner review.
- **First Strike:** Upload a pitch deck PDF, pick the stage, and receive a scored analysis with Verified/Quick Win/Gap breakdown — in under 60 seconds, no signup required.
- **KUI:** 3+ deck analyses completed (user has integrated it into their screening workflow, not just kicked the tires).
- **Core Offer:**
  - Result: Structured, investor-grade pitch deck evaluations that save 80%+ of screening time, with actionable recommendations separated into "deck edits" and "business changes"
  - Advantage: The only tool with a 45-question stage-gated checklist and Verified/Quick Win/Gap classification — built from real investor evaluation work, not simulated
  - Assurance: Free to use, no signup for first analysis, decks ephemeral (deleted after analysis), results in under 60 seconds

## First Strike Path
1. Landing page — live demo area is the hero; user sees an example analysis immediately (value before signup)
2. Upload PDF + pick stage — single form: drag-drop deck, select Pre-Seed/Seed/Series A+ (1 click)
3. Processing — ~60 seconds, progress indicator
4. See scored analysis — interactive results page with full Verified/Quick Win/Gap breakdown
Clicks to value: 2

Signup (email via magic link) gates the second analysis, not the first.

## Decisions
- **Framework:** Hono + htmx
- **Deploy:** Cloudflare Workers (+ Pages for static assets)
- **Auth:** Magic Link via Resend
- **Analytics:** PostHog (funnels, session replay, cohort analysis)
- **Database:** Cloudflare D1 (SQLite) + R2 (object storage for PDFs and result HTML)
- **Intro Video:** Screenshot GIF (autoplay walkthrough of upload → results flow)
- **Template Split:** 80/20 (template: page structure, auth, analytics, deploy. Custom: core feature, results page, demo data, VC copy)

## Notes

- COGS are ~$0.08-0.12 per analysis (Sonnet with caching). Margins are healthy at any price point.
